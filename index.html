Eat Now, Pay Later

Order your favorite food and pay later with EMI options with instant approval.

What is Khana On EMI?

Khana On EMI is a flexible meal subscription service where you can enjoy delicious food now and pay in easy installments later.

How does the EMI option work?

Instead of paying the full amount upfront, you can split your bill into smaller, manageable payments over time.

What kind of meals are available?

We offer a wide range of options including daily meals, family packs, and special occasion menus. Vegetarian and non-vegetarian choices are available.

Is there a minimum order value for EMI?

Yes, EMI is available on orders above a certain value. The exact minimum depends on the plan you choose

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel anytime. However, cancellation policies may vary depending on your EMI plan.

How do I track my payments?

You can log in to your account dashboard to view upcoming payments, due dates, and completed transactions.

Is delivery included in the EMI plan?

Yes, delivery charges are included unless specified otherwise for certain locations.
